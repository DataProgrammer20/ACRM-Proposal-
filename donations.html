Artifacts:

The American Computer Museum has an extensive collection and due to logistical and space limitations, it is very selective as to what items will be accepted as donations towards its collections. Please contact the museum FIRST to find out if the object(s) you wish to donate will be accepted. The museum is not responsible for any items left on its premises without such approval.
